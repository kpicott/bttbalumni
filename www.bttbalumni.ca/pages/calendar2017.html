70th Reunion Calendar of Events

As every band member knows the itinerary of a tour is only final when it's over, so here is the always-tentative order of events for the weekend. Central Arena will be our headquarters for the weekend starting on Friday so if in doubt go there first.

Saturday 15 April 2017
Early-Bird Deadline, prices go up after this date!
Wednesday 14 June 2017
  • First concert practice for alumni playing in the concert-in-the-park. 7-9pm at the Music Centre with the BTTB.
  • Alumni intending on sitting in on the concert should let us know what part they will be playing and download all music link:(#concert2017,on the website)
Friday 16 June 2017
  • Registration at Central Arena starting at 4pm.
  • Marching band practice, Central Arena from 7-9pm.
  • Alumni participating in the parade should download all music link:(#parade2017,on the website) to practice and we will break them down to just 3 for Saturday.
  • Pizza and beverages in Central Arena after the practice
Saturday 17 June 2017
  • Late registration at Central Arena from 9:30am to 11am.
  • Shuttle buses will be available at Burlington Mall for 10am, returning after the parade
  • Park at the mall lot facing Guelph Line, north of Kelsey's driveway (the old Target, Zellers, Bay, Dominion, ...)
  • There is no parking at the Music Centre or Central Arena
  • link:(#store2017,Parade) - Assemble at Central Arena by 10:30am (streets close then)
  • You can leave your music case in Central Arena's Auditorium
  • Shuttle bus to the start of the parade from Victoria Street (East of Brant) for those with the Top Hats, Juniors, BTTB
  • Preferred dress is Reunion Shirt, Hat, and Khaki shorts or pants
  • Parade steps off at 11am, we are the last unit.
  • link:(#store2017,Social Event) at Central Arena and final registration - doors open at 6:30pm
Sunday 18 June 2017
  • Pre-concert rehearsal at the band shell, 2:00pm
  • Arrive at 6:00pm to set up for the concert in the park.
  • Concert in the park with the BTTB, 7:00pm at the bandshell
  • Lawnchairs or beach blankets recommended for spectators.
Monday 19 June 2017
