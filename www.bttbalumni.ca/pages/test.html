For the first time ever, drumline alumni will have the opportunity to participate in the Concert in the Park! For alumni from the drumline who wish to participate, there is the option to perform in a part of the BTTB drum feature. The music can be downloaded using the link below. Rehearsal times with the drumline will take place at the same time as the rest of the ensemble (Wednesday the 14th and Sunday the 18th).
