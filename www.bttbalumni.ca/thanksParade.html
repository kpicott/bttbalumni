Thanks for Signing Up

You can change your part at any time by returning to the parade page if you wish, but you can only sign up for one part to play.

More information will become available as the parade date draws closer. Don't forget the marching practice Friday night at 7 - you'll want the time to get your legs back in shape, and remember which foot goes forward.

If you need an instrument, Long and McQuade in Burlington is offering a rental program the alumni can take advantage of. Have a look at their brochure to see what would suit your needs.

Interested in playing the Sunday concert as well? link:(#musicSurvey,Click here to take a survey) on your favourite music from band days to include. Music will be available here once it has been selected by Sir Bill.

Know anyone else from the band that has not yet registered? Help us make this the best celebration ever by spreading the word, and don't forget that the fun doesn't end after the celebration. The alumni would like to stay in touch for other great events.
